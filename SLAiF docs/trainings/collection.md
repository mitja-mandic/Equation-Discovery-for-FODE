## Overview

This collection hosts training-oriented SLAIF materials that are intended to be published under the top-level
`/trainings` hierarchy on `edu.slaif.si`.

## Editorial policy

The collection is meant for structured training books, workshop materials, and guided learning resources. Books
should be added as peer directories and may use either explicit front-matter listing or directory discovery, in
line with the Notes content model.
